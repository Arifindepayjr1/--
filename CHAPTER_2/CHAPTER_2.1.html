A paragraph consists of one or more sentences that form a self-contained unit of discourse. The start of a paragraph is indicatedd by a new line.

Text is easier to understand when it split up into units of text. For example, a book may have chapters. Chapters can have subheadings. Under each heading there will be one or more paragraphs.

By default Browser will show each paragraph in the new line with some space between it any subsequent paragraphs
