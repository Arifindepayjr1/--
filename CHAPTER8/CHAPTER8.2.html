for a one-year period from november 2010, the margugame genichiro-inokuma museum of contemporary art (mimoca) will host a cycle of four hiroshi sugimoto exhibitions.

Each will showcase works by the artist thematically contextualized under the headings "Science," "Architecture," "History" and "Religion" so as to present a comprehensive panorama of the artist's oeuvre.

Hours : 10:00 - 18:00 (no addmittance after 17:30)
