The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense planting , and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, When stylized versions were form as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estates gardens

The earliest cottage gardens were moer pratical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
